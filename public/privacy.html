Privacy notice

TonRings does not request or store wallet seed phrases or private keys. Wallet addresses, one-time proof data, session identifiers, and NFT ownership evidence may be processed to provide authentication and enchantment functions. Sessions are stored as hashes and expire automatically. Blockchain transactions and NFT ownership are public on TON. Third-party infrastructure such as TON Center, hosting providers, PostgreSQL hosting, and IPFS providers may process technical request data under their own policies.

Contact the project owner before production launch to add the final legal contact, retention periods, and jurisdiction-specific disclosures.
