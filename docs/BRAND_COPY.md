## GitHub description

Non-custodial TON NFT enchantment platform with TonConnect verification, atomic ring-to-Diamond binding, PostgreSQL persistence, deterministic collection generation and IPFS automation.

## Getgems collection description

TonRings is a collection of digital championship rings created to carry a moment, identity and legacy on-chain.

Each ring has its own material, engraving, gem signal and rarity profile. Eligible collectors can pair a ring with a verified TON Diamond to activate a non-custodial visual enchantment. Both NFTs remain safely inside the collector's wallet while TonRings records the relationship through ownership verification.

**Collect the ring. Prove the Diamond. Activate the legacy.**

TonRings is an independent project and is not affiliated with FIFA, TON Foundation, TON Diamonds, Getgems, football teams or players.

## Attention principles

1. Lead with identity and emotion, not infrastructure.
2. Explain non-custody immediately after the emotional promise.
3. Use one action per screen.
4. Reveal technical proof progressively.
5. Keep copy short enough to scan on a phone.
6. Never imply official affiliation or guaranteed financial value.
7. Describe ownership and rarity accurately, without artificial urgency.
